Privacy & Terms

This page explains what happens to the information you share on b4thecall.com. It covers this website only — the B4C application has its own terms provided to departments and associations that use it.

What we collect

The only information this site asks for is what you type into the demo request form: your name, department or association, email, phone number, and department size. We don't use advertising trackers, and we don't run analytics that build a profile of you across the web.

How we use it

We use those details for one thing: to contact you about the demo you asked for and to follow up about B4C. That's it. We don't sell your information, we don't rent it, and we don't share it with third parties for their own marketing.

How the form works

The demo form opens your own email application with the details filled in, so your request reaches us as an ordinary email. If that changes to a hosted form in the future, this page will be updated to name the service that processes it.

How long we keep it

We keep your request as long as we're in conversation about B4C, and a reasonable time after, for our records. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

Your choices

  • Ask what we have on file for you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete it.
  • Ask us to stop contacting you — we'll stop.

For any of these, email hello@b4thecall.com.

Terms of use

This site is provided as-is for information about B4C. The pricing, availability, and vertical statuses shown here may change as the product develops. Nothing on this page is a binding offer or contract; the terms that govern using the B4C application are provided separately to each department and association.
